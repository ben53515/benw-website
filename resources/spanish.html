Websites

itsnachotime.com

Amazing course for intermediate levels+ and community aspect is very valuable. Amazing methodical tips for learning Spanish
spanishpodcast.net

Podcast for learners (A2/B1 level minimum in my opinion), gives great tips for learning, natural listening comprehension, grammar explanations and colloquial phrases (Spain)
nohaytospodcast.com

Another great podcast, great for intermediate levels+, includes clear grammatical explanations and listening comprehension (Mexico)
RTVE Playz

Contains a large variety of interesting programs made for natives (Spanish TV). Would recommend 'La Madriguera' which talks about topics such as Education and Science
mimicmethod.com

Great for improving pronunciation, personally haven't tried the course but signing up for email provides great tips and free resources (his sample lessons can be found here)
muyinteresante.es

Great Website with articles on a range of topics for reading practice, aimed at natives so recommend for higher levels

Channels for Learning Spanish

Dreaming Spanish

Probably the best Channel out there currently for Spanish Listening Comprehension Complete Beginner-Upper Intermediate -- HIGHLY Recommend
NachoTime Spanish

Also an Amazing Channel, related to Website as mentioned but also provides challenging interactive videos for intermediate level+ that really help you to improve
Bilingüe Blogs

Very different kind of Spanish spoken here with Dominican Spanish, gives out great tips and I recommend the Listening Podcast-style Videos, recommend for all levels, listening for intermediate+
Español con Juan

Amazing grammar explanations, very entertaining the whole time, explanations with full context so if you're looking for something explained come here, recommend for upper-beginners and intermediate+
SpanishPodcast.net

Linked to aforementioned website, videos with awesome tips for learning and explanations, recommend for intermediate+
Why Not Spanish?

Another different accent here (Colombian), very clear explanations and well formatted videos to help you learn vocab, for all levels, recommend the listening activity videos especially
Extra en Español

Program Made for students of spanish, so stupid its hilarious but also useful to learn from if you're a beginner

General Useful Language learning resources

Italki

Probably the best place on the internet to find online language teachers for range of prices and also language exchange partners
Tandem

Amazing app for finding Exchange Partners, and talking with a variety of people from around the world
Hellotalk

great app for finding language exchange partners and also communicating with wide range of people in target language to improve writing skills, natives to help correct you
Pimsleur

Great for starting to learn a language with no prior knowledge, would recommend to learn the basics (first 1 or 2 units) and get used to the feel of the language, speaking and understanding
DeepL Translator

Best translator, much more natural than Google Translate
Linguee Dictionary

finds wide range of example texts with searched word which have been translated by speakers of both languages so are very accurate. Very good to grasp meaning of a word/phrase
Forvo - Pronunciation

Helps you find the correct pronunciation of a word in a variety of accents
