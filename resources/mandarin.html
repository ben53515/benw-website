Channels for Learning Mandarin

Hit Chinese

Comprehensible input videos and Chinese explanations, lots of useful and enjoyable content
Unconventional Chinese with Keren

TPRS Comprehensible Input Listening practice in Chinese, already a lot of useful content and more being added consistently
Shianguu Hsieh

Some amazing Comprehensible input videos for beginners, highly recommend to start
SyS Mandarin

Uses authentic content and explains it thoroughly such as Stories, Speeches, Movies, giving lots of useful listening practice and vocabulary

General Useful Language Learning resources

Italki

Probably the best place on the internet to find online language teachers for range of prices and also language exchange partners
Tandem

Amazing app for finding Exchange Partners, and talking with a variety of people from around the world
Hellotalk

great app for finding language exchange partners and also communicating with wide range of people in target language to improve writing skills, natives to help correct you
Pimsleur

Great for starting to learn a language with no prior knowledge, would recommend to learn the basics (first 1 or 2 units) and get used to the feel of the language, speaking and understanding
DeepL Translator

Best translator, much more natural than Google Translate, unfortunately does not support PinYin
Linguee Dictionary

finds wide range of example texts with searched word which have been translated by speakers of both languages so are very accurate. Very good to grasp meaning of a word/phrase
